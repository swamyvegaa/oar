Pair Very Early Hans Wegner Swivel Chair for Johannes Hansen

This is an authentic pair of 1955 Hans Wegner Swivel Chairs produced by Johannes Hansen. This is the earliest edition with the Bakelite wheels on the Bassick casters. (Later editions came with the rolling ball casters). Both chairs are completely original and have been with the same family since new. I decided not to touch, restore or clean these in any way because I know whoever buys them will want to do this themselves.
